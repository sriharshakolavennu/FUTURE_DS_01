##BUSINESS SALES ANALYSIS FROM E-COMMERCE DATA – POWER BI DASHBOARD

##Project Overview  
This project analyzes E-commerce business sales data using a Power BI dashboard.  
The dashboard provides insights into total sales, quantity sold, orders, customers, product performance, country-wise sales, order status, and sales trends over time.  
It helps understand business performance, customer behavior, and sales distribution to support data-driven decision-making.

##Dataset Information  
● Source: E-Commerce Sales Dataset  
● Format: CSV / Excel  
● Records: Transaction-level data  
● Granularity: Invoice Date, Product, Country, Customer, Order Status  

Key Columns Used  
● InvoiceDate – Order date  
● Country – Customer country  
● Product Code – Product identifier  
● Quantity Sold – Number of items sold  
● Price – Product price  
● Total Sales – Revenue generated  
● Customer ID – Unique customer identifier  
● Order Status – Completed / Cancelled  

Objectives of the Project  
● Analyze overall business sales performance  
● Track monthly and yearly sales trends  
● Identify top-performing products  
● Analyze country-wise sales distribution  
● Understand order completion vs cancellation  
● Compare actual sales with sales targets  

Dashboard Features  
● KPI Cards  
  ● Total Sales  
  ● Total Quantity Sold  
  ● Total Orders  
  ● Total Customers  
  ● Average Orders  

Visualizations Used  
● Line Chart – Monthly Sales Trend by Year  
● Bar Chart – Top 10 Products by Sales  
● Column Chart – Sales by Country  
● Donut Chart – Order Status Distribution  
● Gauge Chart – Sales Target Achievement

Data Cleaning & Preparation (Step-by-Step)

● Removed missing and duplicate records  
● Converted data types (Date, Numbers)

● Applied filters and slicers  

Measures Created  
● Total Sales  
● Total Quantity

● Total Customers

Key Insights :  
● Sales show seasonal growth across months  
● A few products contribute significantly to total revenue  
● One country dominates overall sales volume  
● Most orders are completed successfully  
● Sales target is partially achieved, indicating growth potential
